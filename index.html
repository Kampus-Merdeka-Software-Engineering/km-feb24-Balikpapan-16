DATA SUPERSTORE

The Superstore dataset is a well-known dataset for data analysis and visualization, and it is frequently used to teach and demonstrate business intelligence solutions such as Tableau, Power BI, and other platforms. It covers information on sales, profits, clients, items, and shipping details for a fictitious retail office supply company. The dataset can be connected using keys like Order ID or Customer ID and is often delivered as an Excel or CSV file with numerous tables or sheets.
